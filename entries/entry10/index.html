Ingredients
1/4 cabbage finely sliced
500 gr chopped kabocha squash
4 cloves minced garlic
1 small onion sliced
2 scallions chopped
5 shiitake mushrooms soaked and thinkly sliced
200gr dried vermicelli noodles
3 eggs whisked
1/4c dried shrimp
Instructions

1. Prep ingredients.

2. Preheat pan. Add cooking oil and saute shiitake mushrooms until lightly golden brown.

3. Add onion, garlic, dried shrimp. Saute until aromatic. About 2 minutes.

4. Push everything to the side of the pan. Scrambled the eggs mix everything in togehter.

5. Add pre-soaked vermicelli noodles followed by the seasonings. Stir to combine. You can use a tong to help you to move things around so they are evenly combined.

6. Add the shredded pumpkin and toss everything to combine.

7. Cover with a lid and let them cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the pumpkin are soften but not mushy.

8. Then uncover the lid and give everything a stir.

9. Add the green parts of green onion and toss everything to combine. Taste and add seasonings as needed.

10. Serve immediately
