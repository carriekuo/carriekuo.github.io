Recipe of the Week:

Braised Pork Over Rice

( 肉燥饭 )

Ingredients
Instruction
Reviews

INGREDIENTS:

minced garlic
diced onion
minced ginger
ground pork
soy sauce
rice wine
sugar
white pepper
fried shallots
scallion

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Heat some oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the ginger and shallots for 1-2 minutes, or until the shallots turn translucent.
  2. Increase the heat to high. Add 1 additional tablespoon of oil, along with the ground pork. Cook until the meat is opaque.
  3. When the pork is cooked, stir in the rice wine. Add the sugar, soy sauce, white pepper, five spice powder, and 2 cups of water
  4. Bring to a boil. Once boiling, cover, reduce the heat to medium/medium-low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Stir in the scallions and salt to taste.
  6. Ladle the pork over rice.
