Japanese Curry Rice

Ingredients
curry roux blocks
2 carrots, chopped
2 potatoes, chopped
1 onion, sliced
1 lb of meat (chicken), diced
chicken broth
Instructions

1. In a large pot, heat some oil and add the diced meat. Cook until browned.

2. Add the sliced onions and cook until they become translucent.

3. Add the chopped carrots and potatoes. Stir-fry for a few minutes.

4. Pour in the water and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

5. Break the curry roux blocks into smaller pieces and add them to the pot. Stir until the roux is completely dissolved.

6. Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the curry thickens.

7. Serve the Japanese curry over hot cooked rice.
